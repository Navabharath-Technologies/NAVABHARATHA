Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for your business needs

Zed Certification

ZED Certification (Zero Defect Zero Effect) is a Government of India initiative that helps MSMEs improve product quality and reduce environmental impact. It certifies that a business follows high-quality manufacturing practices while being environment-friendly, efficient, and compliant with standards.

Learn More

Zed Consulting

ZED Consulting is a professional service that helps businesses prepare for and achieve ZED Certification by guiding them through process improvements, documentation, compliance requirements, and best practices. It supports MSMEs in improving quality, efficiency, and sustainability as per ZED standards.

Learn More

Zed Assessment

ZED Assessment is the evaluation process where a business’s quality systems, processes, and environmental practices are reviewed to check their readiness and compliance with ZED Certification standards. It identifies gaps and improvement areas before certification.

Learn More
