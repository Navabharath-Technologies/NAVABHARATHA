Overview

ZED Assessment is a critical step in your certification journey. It involves a detailed evaluation of your business processes, quality standards, and environmental impact against the ZED maturity model.

Our assessment services help you identify gaps in your current system and provide a roadmap to bridge them, ensuring you meet the requirements for Bronze, Silver, or Gold certification.

Assessment Process

  • Self-Assessment: Initial review based on online parameters.
  • Desktop Assessment: Verification of documents and evidence submitted online.
  • Site Assessment: For Silver and Gold levels, physical verification of the facility.
  • Report Generation: Detailed report highlighting strengths and areas for improvement.

Benefits of Assessment

A rigorous assessment not only prepares you for certification but also highlights operational inefficiencies, helping you reduce waste, improve quality, and increase profitability.
